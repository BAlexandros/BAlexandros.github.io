I am Alexandros, a computational physics graduate student with endless curiosity on all matters related to Physics and Computer Science.
This website acts as my online CV, where I give some information about myself and showcase projects I've completed or currently working on.

Some things about me:

I was born in 1996 in Thessaloniki, the second-largest city in Greece. I finished highschool at the 17th Lyceum of Thessaloniki, and enrolled in the Physics Department of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki in 2014. In 2019 I completed my undergraduate studies and in early 2020 I had my first research published. I then enrolled in the Computational Physics MSc program of Aristotle's University of Thessaloniki, which I am currently attending remotely on the weekends.

I am a Canadian by descent, and I now live in Kitchener, Ontario.
